Cricket

The Gentleman's Game

About

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It requires strategy, skill, and teamwork to score runs while defending your wicket.

Basic Rules

  • Each team gets to bat and bowl
  • Score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets
  • Batsman is out if bowled, caught, LBW, run out, or stumped
  • Match consists of overs (6 balls per over)
  • Team with most runs wins

Tournament Format

The tournament will be played in T20 format with 8 teams competing. Matches will be held during school hours with knockout rounds leading to the grand finale.

Requirements

  • Sports uniform required
  • Cricket shoes (optional but recommended)
  • Protective gear will be provided
  • Team must have 11 players minimum

Football

The Beautiful Game

About

Football is the world's most popular sport, combining skill, strategy, and teamwork. Score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's net using any part of your body except your hands.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 11 players each
  • Match duration: 40 minutes (20 min per half)
  • No hands allowed (except goalkeeper in penalty area)
  • Free kicks awarded for fouls
  • Offside rule applies
  • Yellow and red cards for misconduct

Tournament Format

League format followed by semi-finals and finals. All matches will be played on the main football ground. Group stage matches will determine top 4 teams for knockout rounds.

Requirements

  • Football boots or sports shoes
  • Shin guards mandatory
  • Sports uniform required
  • Water bottle recommended

Basketball

Fast-Paced Court Action

About

Basketball is a dynamic team sport where players score by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. It requires agility, precision, and excellent coordination.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 5 players each
  • Score by shooting ball through hoop (2 or 3 points)
  • Dribble or pass the ball to move it
  • No running with the ball (traveling violation)
  • Fouls result in free throws
  • 24-second shot clock

Tournament Format

Double elimination tournament format. Games will be 4 quarters of 8 minutes each. Top teams will advance to championship round.

Requirements

  • Basketball shoes required
  • Sports uniform
  • Knee pads recommended
  • Team must have minimum 5 players

Badminton

Speed and Precision

About

Badminton is a racket sport where players hit a shuttlecock across a net. It's one of the fastest racket sports, requiring quick reflexes and strategic play.

Basic Rules

  • Singles (1v1) or Doubles (2v2) format
  • Games played to 21 points
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Shuttlecock must not touch the ground
  • Server serves diagonally to opponent
  • Best of 3 games wins the match

Tournament Format

Both singles and doubles events will be held. Knockout format from round of 16 onwards. All matches are best of 3 games.

Requirements

  • Racket (can be borrowed from school)
  • Sports shoes with good grip
  • Sports uniform required
  • Sweatband recommended

Athletics

Track and Field Excellence

About

Athletics encompasses various track and field events testing speed, endurance, strength, and jumping ability. Compete individually in multiple events.

Events

  • Sprints: 100m, 200m, 400m
  • Middle Distance: 800m, 1500m
  • Relay: 4x100m, 4x400m
  • Jumping: Long Jump, High Jump
  • Throwing: Shot Put, Discus

Rules & Format

Students can participate in maximum 3 individual events plus relays. Heats and finals format for track events. Best attempt counts for field events. Points awarded based on performance for overall championship.

Requirements

  • Running shoes/spikes
  • Sports uniform
  • Proper warm-up mandatory
  • Medical clearance recommended

Volleyball

Power at the Net

About

Volleyball is a team sport where two teams hit a ball over a net trying to make it land in the opponent's court. It requires coordination, jumping ability, and teamwork.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 6 players each
  • Maximum 3 touches per side
  • Games played to 25 points (win by 2)
  • Rally scoring system
  • Best of 5 sets wins the match
  • Rotation after winning serve

Tournament Format

Pool play followed by single elimination playoffs. All matches best of 3 sets. Championship match will be best of 5 sets.

Requirements

  • Volleyball shoes or court shoes
  • Knee pads mandatory
  • Sports uniform required
  • Team must have 6-8 players
