Cricket

The Gentleman's Game

About

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It requires strategy, skill, and teamwork to score runs while defending your wicket.

Basic Rules

  • Each team gets to bat and bowl
  • Score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets
  • Batsman is out if bowled, caught, LBW, run out, or stumped
  • Match consists of overs (6 balls per over)
  • Team with most runs wins

Tournament Format

The tournament will be played in T20 format with teams competing. Matches will be held during the event with knockout rounds leading to the grand finale.

Football

The Beautiful Game

About

Football is the world's most popular sport, combining skill, strategy, and teamwork. Score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's net.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 11 players each
  • No hands allowed (except goalkeeper in penalty area)
  • Free kicks awarded for fouls
  • Offside rule applies
  • Yellow and red cards for misconduct

Basketball

Fast-Paced Court Action

About

Basketball is a dynamic team sport where players score by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. Requires agility, precision, and coordination.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 5 players each
  • Score by shooting ball through hoop (2 or 3 points)
  • Dribble or pass to move the ball
  • No running with the ball (traveling violation)
  • Fouls result in free throws

Badminton

Speed and Precision

About

Badminton is a racket sport where players hit a shuttlecock across a net. It's one of the fastest racket sports, requiring quick reflexes and strategic play.

Basic Rules

  • Singles (1v1) or Doubles (2v2) format
  • Games played to 21 points
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Shuttlecock must not touch the ground
  • Server serves diagonally to opponent
  • Best of 3 games wins the match

Volleyball

Power at the Net

About

Volleyball is a team sport where two teams hit a ball over a net trying to make it land in the opponent's court. Requires coordination, jumping ability, and teamwork.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 6 players each
  • Maximum 3 touches per side
  • Games played to 25 points (win by 2)
  • Rally scoring system
  • Best of 5 sets wins the match
  • Rotation after winning serve

Tug of War

Ultimate Strength Challenge

About

Tug of War is a classic team sport where two teams pull on opposite ends of a rope. The team that pulls the rope a certain distance wins. It's the ultimate test of strength, strategy, and teamwork.

Basic Rules

  • Teams of 8-10 members each
  • Teams pull on opposite ends of a rope
  • Win by pulling the center marker past a designated point
  • Best of 3 rounds format
  • All team members must maintain grip on rope
  • No sitting or falling allowed

Tournament Format

Knockout tournament format. Each match is best of 3 pulls. Teams must coordinate their pulling rhythm and maintain strong footing throughout the competition.

Ludo

Classic Board Game

About

Ludo is a classic board game that combines strategy and luck. Players race their four tokens from start to finish according to dice rolls. A game that's simple to learn but challenging to master!

Basic Rules

  • 2-4 players per game
  • Roll a 6 to bring token out of home
  • Move tokens according to dice roll
  • Capture opponent tokens by landing on same space
  • First player to get all 4 tokens to finish wins
  • Safe zones protect your tokens

Tournament Format

Single elimination tournament with 4-player matches in early rounds. Semi-finals and finals will determine the ultimate Ludo champion. Strategy and luck combine for exciting gameplay!

Chess

The Game of Kings

About

Chess is a strategic board game played between two players on a checkered board. It's a battle of minds where players must think several moves ahead to outmaneuver their opponent and checkmate the king.

Basic Rules

  • Two players with 16 pieces each
  • Each piece moves in specific patterns
  • Capture opponent's pieces by moving to their square
  • Protect your king while threatening opponent's
  • Win by checkmate (king cannot escape attack)
  • Draw if stalemate or insufficient material

Tournament Format

Swiss system tournament with time controls. Players will compete in multiple rounds with chess clocks. Top players advance to knockout finals. Think carefully, plan strategically!

FIFA

Virtual Football Championship

About

FIFA is the world's most popular football video game. Master controls, team tactics, and gameplay mechanics to dominate the virtual pitch. Show off your gaming skills and football knowledge!

Basic Rules

  • 1v1 matches on PlayStation/Xbox
  • Choose from top international teams
  • Standard match duration: 6 minutes per half

  • Winner determined by score at full time

Double elimination bracket format. Group stage followed by knockout rounds. All matches played under tournament settings. Grand final will determine the FIFA champion!

Tekken

Fighting Game Mastery

About

Tekken is one of the most iconic fighting games in esports history. Master complex combos, juggle techniques, and character matchups to become the ultimate fighter. Every frame counts!

Basic Rules

  • 1v1 fighting matches
  • Best of 3 rounds per match
  • Character selection before each match
  • No coaching during matches
  • Standard tournament rules apply
  • Respect your opponent at all times

Tournament Format

Single elimination bracket with best of 3 matches. Semi-finals and finals will be best of 5. Master your character's move list and adapt to different playstyles to claim victory!
